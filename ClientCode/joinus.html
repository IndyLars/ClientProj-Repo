Member Benefits

Becoming a member of Studio 105 comes with various benefits only available to students. These benefits will positively impact your social, professional, and personal life as a student and photographer.

  • - You have the opportunity to socialize and get to know new people
  • - You can build a community of people with similar interests
  • - You'll develop new skills within the field of photography
  • - You'll get to work with a diverse group of people
  • - You can start building your social network
  • - Available leadership positions
  • - Boost your resume with organizational involvement
  • - You'll have access to high-quality equipment, including exceptional photo printers, a state-of-the-art portrait studio (105!), and Adobe Creative Cloud applications (Lightroom Classic, Photoshop, Bridge, etc,..)
